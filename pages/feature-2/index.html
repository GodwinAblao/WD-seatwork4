Battle Royale Mode

As the game environment shrinks, players race to be the last individual or group standing while collecting weapons and equipment.

Gulag Mechanic

Players can win a 1v1 duel in the Gulag to have a chance to respawn after being eliminated. This special feature gives participants another opportunity to rejoin the game.

Cross-Platform Play

By enabling cross-platform play, Warzone opens up the game to a wider audience by enabling players on several gaming platforms (PC, PlayStation, Xbox) to compete together.

Loudout Drops

Gamers have the ability to summon loadout drops, which grant them access to their personalized loadouts. By enabling players to employ their favorite weapons and equipment, this element gives the gaming a more personalized and strategic feel.
